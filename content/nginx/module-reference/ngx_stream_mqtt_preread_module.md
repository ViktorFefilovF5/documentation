## `mqtt_preread`

**Syntax:** `on` | `off`

**Default:** off

**Contexts:** `stream, server`

Enables extracting information from the MQTT CONNECT message at
the [preread](https://nginx.org/en/docs/stream/stream_processing.html#preread_phase) phase.
